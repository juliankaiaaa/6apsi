# 4. Reflection Journal

**Time: 30 to 45 minutes.** The first three documents look forward at your
project. This one looks back at your **journey through this course so far** -
from JavaScript fundamentals, through React, to styling and design. Writing it
down is how you notice what you actually learned (and what is still shaky) before
you lean on it in the build.

Write in full sentences and be honest. There are no wrong answers here; a vague,
generic entry is worth less than a short specific one. Aim for roughly **400 to
600 words** total across the sections below.

## What to keep

This journal is for you first, but you will hand it in as the fourth part of your
planning submission. Keep it honest and concrete. When the build gets hard, the
"what is still shaky" and "how I get unstuck" sections are the notes that help
future-you the most.
